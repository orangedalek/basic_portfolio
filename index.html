About Me

I grew up in Wisconsin, went to college in Indianapolis, and moved to San Francisco in 2002. I spent most of 2016 in New Orleans, where I dressed up as a pirate and sold absinthe to tourists. In December, I moved back to the Bay Area and am now living in Oakland.

When I’m not in class, I’m a freelance writer. I also enjoy riding my bikes (the pedal kind, not the vroom vroom kind,) cooking, reading books with pictures, highbrow beer, live music, reading books without pictures, comedy, lowbrow wine, smelly cheeses (ask me about the cheese factory century,) cats, record collecting, cooking, and non-smelly cheeses.
